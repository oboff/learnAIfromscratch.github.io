Learn AI From Scratch

Welcome

This is an ongoing workshop to build from scratch the basic background needed to attempt introductory books and courses in AI related topics such as NLP or DL or autonomous agents. The idea is that you take the software and math workshop as needed until whenever you can start to understand some of the above courses or content in the ai workshop not that you have to wait to finish one until you can do the other.

Topics are seperated into their own individual workshop, I'm going to do all three at the same time, a little each day, since Erik Demaine thinks we should "timeshare the brain" and pipeline topics in parallel.
